Everything you need

One platform for your
entire financial operation

Vero replaces the tangle of spreadsheets and disconnected tools finance teams rely on — built for the manager who needs to run the close without a consultant.

Automated Reconciliation

Vero pulls transactions from your bank, payment processors, and ERP and matches them automatically. Discrepancies surface in a single flagged queue — no more hunting through spreadsheet rows. Most teams reconcile in under two hours.

Approval Workflows

Build multi-step approval chains for invoices, expenses, and journal entries — no rules to write manually. Approvers can approve or reject directly from their inbox. Every decision is logged with a timestamp and reason.

Real-Time Reporting

Vero generates your P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statement the moment transactions post — not at month-end. Share live report links with your board instead of exporting to PDF every time something changes.

Multi-Entity Support

Manage multiple subsidiaries, legal entities, or cost centers from a single Vero account. Consolidate financials across entities with one click — full intercompany eliminations handled automatically.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action in Vero — every edit, approval, import, and export — is logged against the user who made it. Audit-ready by default. No more reconstructing what happened and when before an audit or board review.

Integrations

Vero connects natively to QuickBooks, NetSuite, Stripe, Gusto, and 40+ other tools your team already uses. Two-way sync keeps your data consistent across systems without manual exports or custom scripts.

From five days to one

Most of that time is reconciliation — matching transactions by hand across systems that don't talk to each other.
