Everything you need

One platform for the entire financial close

Replace the tangle of spreadsheets, inboxes, and disconnected tools your finance team has been making work for years.

Automated Reconciliation

Vero pulls transactions from your bank, payment processors, and ERP and matches them automatically. Discrepancies surface in a single flagged queue — no more hunting through spreadsheet rows.

Approval Workflows

Build multi-step approval chains for invoices, expenses, and journal entries without writing a single rule manually. Approvers get notified by email and can approve or reject directly from their inbox.

Real-Time Reporting

Vero generates your P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow statement the moment transactions are posted — not at month-end. Share live report links with your board or investors instead of exporting to PDF every time something changes.

Multi-Entity Support

Manage multiple subsidiaries, legal entities, or cost centers from a single Vero account. Consolidate financials across entities with one click, with full intercompany eliminations handled automatically.

Complete Audit Trail

Every action in Vero — every edit, approval, import, and export — is logged against the user who made it. Audit-ready by default. No more reconstructing what happened and when before an audit.

Integrations

Vero connects natively to QuickBooks, NetSuite, Stripe, Gusto, and 40+ other tools your team already uses. Two-way sync keeps your data consistent across systems without manual exports or custom scripts.

How Vero works

Built around how finance teams actually operate

Month-End Close

From five days to one

The average finance team at a 200-person company spends four to five working days closing the books each month. Most of that time is reconciliation — matching transactions by hand across systems that don't talk to each other. Vero connects to every source automatically and runs the matching logic in the background. By the time your team opens the platform on close day, 95% of the work is already done.

  • Matches transactions across bank, ERP, and processors in minutes
  • Flags only the exceptions that need human review
  • Full close checklist with sign-off tracking built in
