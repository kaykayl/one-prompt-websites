# One-Prompt Websites

A collection of structured prompts that generate production-ready landing pages from a single paste into any AI coder. No designer. No developer. Just fill in what you know about your product.

---

## How it works

Each template is split into two parts.

**Part 1 — You fill this in.**
Your company name, what your product does, your features, your testimonials. No design knowledge needed. If you know your product, you can fill it in.

**Part 2 — Do not touch.**
This is the design system for that template style. It tells the AI exactly how the page should look, feel, and behave. It's already written. Leave it alone.

Once Part 1 is filled, copy the entire file and paste it into Claude, Cursor, or any AI coder. That's the whole process.

## Quick start

1. Open the prompt file for the template you want
2. Fill in every `[bracket]` in Part 1 — nothing else
3. Copy the entire file, top to bottom
4. Paste it into [Claude](https://claude.ai) or your AI coder of choice
5. Ask it to output a single HTML file
6. Open the file in your browser

That's it.

---

## What's in Part 1

You'll be asked for:

- Your company name and tagline
- A plain-language description of what your product does
- Who your buyers are
- Your CTA button text and trust signals
- Your hero headline and subheadline
- Up to 6 features with short descriptions
- 3 product spotlight sections
- 3 customer testimonials
- 4 proof metrics
- A closing headline

No hex codes. No font names. No layout decisions. Just your product.
